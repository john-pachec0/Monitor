## Description of Service

Untwist is a personal journaling and thought-reframing tool based on Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) techniques. The App helps you:
- Capture anxious thoughts when they occur
- Schedule time to review and reframe those thoughts
- Learn about cognitive distortions
- Practice evidence-based CBT techniques

### When to Seek Professional Help

If you are experiencing any of the following, **please seek professional help immediately:**
- Thoughts of self-harm or suicide
- Severe depression or anxiety that interferes with daily life
- Panic attacks or overwhelming distress
- Symptoms of psychosis or dissociation
- Substance abuse issues
- Any mental health emergency

**Crisis Resources:**
- **988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline:** Call or text 988 (US)
- **Crisis Text Line:** Text HOME to 741741 (US)
