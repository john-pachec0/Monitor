# Privacy Policy for Monitor

**Last Updated: November 15, 2024**

## Overview

Monitor is a privacy-first eating disorder recovery tracking app designed to help individuals on their recovery journey. Your privacy and data security are our top priorities.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your data on external servers.**

All data you enter in Monitor is:
- Stored **locally on your device only**
- Never transmitted over the internet
- Never shared with third parties
- Never used for analytics or tracking
- Fully controlled by you

## What Data is Stored Locally

Monitor stores the following information locally on your device:
- Meal entries (time, type, location, portion size, photos)
- Emotional context (feelings before, during, and after eating)
- Behavior tracking (personal assessments, exercise, etc.)
- Personal notes and comments
- App settings and preferences

## Data Security

- All data is encrypted at rest using iOS device-level encryption
- Data remains on your device and is protected by your device passcode/Face ID/Touch ID
- Optional Face ID protection adds an additional layer of security within the app
- If you delete the app, all data is permanently removed from your device

## Permissions

Monitor requests the following permissions:

### Camera (Optional)
- **Purpose**: To take photos of meals for your personal recovery journal
- **Usage**: Photos are stored locally on your device only
- **Control**: You can decline camera access; the app functions without it

### Photo Library (Optional)
- **Purpose**: To select existing photos of meals for your recovery journal
- **Usage**: Photos remain on your device only
- **Control**: You can decline photo library access; the app functions without it

### Notifications (Optional)
- **Purpose**: To send local reminders for meal tracking (if enabled)
- **Usage**: Notifications are generated locally on your device
- **Control**: You can disable notifications at any time in iOS Settings

### Face ID (Optional)
- **Purpose**: To protect your private recovery data within the app
- **Usage**: Authentication happens locally on your device
- **Control**: You can disable Face ID protection in app settings

## Third-Party Services

**Monitor does not use any third-party services, analytics, or tracking tools.**

There are:
- No advertising networks
- No analytics frameworks
- No crash reporting services
- No cloud sync services
- No external servers

## Children's Privacy

Monitor is designed for individuals seeking eating disorder recovery support. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you are under 13, please do not use this app without parental guidance.

## Data Portability and Deletion

- **Export**: You can export your data at any time using the in-app PDF export feature
- **Deletion**: All your data is deleted when you uninstall the app
- **No Account**: Since there are no accounts or cloud storage, there is no data to delete from external servers

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app and on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

**Your data belongs to you.** Monitor is designed with privacy as the foundation, not an afterthought. Everything stays on your device, under your control. No exceptions.
